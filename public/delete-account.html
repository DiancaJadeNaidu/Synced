Delete Your Synced Account

If you would like to delete your account and all associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Open the Synced app on your device.
  2. Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account.
  3. Confirm your request to delete your account.

When your account is deleted, the following data will also be removed from our servers:

  • Phone number
  • Email
  • Instagram username
  • Location data

Some data (like analytics or logs) may be retained in anonymized form for up to 30 days.
